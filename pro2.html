African fashion African fashion is known for its bright colors, and it matches the warm climatic conditions of black Africa. The African dress has evolved from loose fashion to tight dresses in a modern way, as shown in the picture.

Guatemalan fashion Like most Latin American costumes, Guatemalan fashion is characterized by its multicolor, as women wear loose and long dresses, especially in carnivals and popular festivals.

mexican traditional costume Known for their large hat, the Mexicans are known as the sombrero or sombrera, which has become a national symbol. It is a hat also worn by folk singers, along with a sleeveless dress that is worn over modern dress.

german bavarian uniform The Bavarian costume (relative to the state of Bavaria) is characterized by elegance and consistency, given that it is one of the ancient European costumes. Variations of Bavarian traditional costume can be seen at the Oktoberfest in Munich. Predominantly green and brown on these costumes.

Russian outfit A Russian woman wearing a folk Russian costume. The traditional Russian costume for women (sarafan) is considered one of the most important traditional costumes in the world, due to its diversity, due to the multiplicity of origins of the Russian peoples. It features bright colors and the iconic kakoshnik hat.

indian outfit Admiration for Indian dress spans decades, and Indian cinema has played a role in promoting it. The sari is the most famous garment for the Indian dress, and it is an expensive and not affordable outfit in India, where there is a large proportion of the poor.
